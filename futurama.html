Futurama, created by Matt Groening fir aired on march 28, 1999. It told the story of a man named Fry who was sent over

Fitingly, the show itself had a rather wild realease history. While it originally aired on the Fox network from 1999-2003, it was canceled by the station.

Comedy Central would be the last network to host the show. Throughout its run the show recieved praise from both critics and fans alike,
